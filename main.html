Summary

Interstellar is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity as Earth becomes uninhabitable. The story explores themes of love, time, sacrifice, and survival across space and dimensions.

Actors

The film stars Matthew McConaughey as Cooper, Anne Hathaway as Brand, Jessica Chastain as Murph, and Michael Caine as Professor Brand. Their performances bring emotional depth to this visually stunning film.
